<p>Divorce doesn't just end a marriage — it can upend everything you thought was stable. Your routines, your role as a father or partner, your sense of where things are headed. For men in the middle of a high-conflict separation, the pressure can come from every direction at once: legal proceedings, financial strain, fractured relationships, and a creeping sense of losing control.</p>

<p>This is a place to slow that down. To get clear on what's actually happening, what you can influence, and what the next steps need to be. Not to process feelings indefinitely — but to stabilize, think clearly, and move forward with purpose.</p>
